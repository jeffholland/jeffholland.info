Milly

Collector of thoughts, ideas, and inspiration. Quickly record your ideas, then arrange them in whatever way makes sense to you.

Just type your thoughts and hit enter, and they immediately get added to your list of entries, along with the exact date and time you submitted them.

Save and load files. Arrange and group entries. Export to csv and txt. Use the Find feature to search your collected thoughts.

Write and save your own color schemes, select from hundreds of fonts, and hide whichever parts of the entry functionality you don't need.
