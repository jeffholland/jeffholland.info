Jeff Holland



My artistic medium involves a process called data-bending.

I manipulate images by converting them to raw data, running various processes on them, and then converting them back into images. These processes manipulate the colors and textures of the image to create something entirely new.

How to use the gallery:

Full screen is strongly recommended. At the top of your browser window, click on View, and then select Full Screen.

You will start at a random image in the gallery. Click the left arrow to view the previous image, right arrow to view the next one. Refresh the page to see another random image.
