Music



This is a complete list of all the music I have ever uploaded to the internet and consider worth listening to.

A note about album art: All album art was created by me (Jeff Holland), unless otherwise stated. For commercially released projects, I have the rights to the images. For non-commercially released projects, I claim no rights.

Cosmonaut

Cosmonaut is an EP of four electronic pop songs. It features contributions from Button Maker and Keygencore. Album art by Matthew Holland.

Genres: Hyperpop, digicore, electronic pop.

Links: Spotify | Apple Music | Soundcloud | Bandcamp

Daydream

Daydream is an album about emerging from the mist of an emotional phase in one's life. Featuring contributions from Maria BC, Austin Allen, and Eric Benoit. Album art by Matthew Holland.

Genres: Ambient pop, dream pop, electronica.

Links: Bandcamp

Air in Heaven

Air in Heaven is an EP of five early works by cat fugue.

Genres: Ambient pop, dream pop, electronica.

Links: Bandcamp

function0

function0 is a noise album, abstractly about computers and how they operate. Its primary medium is FM (Frequency Modulation) synthesis.

Genres: Noise, power electronics

Links: Audiomack
